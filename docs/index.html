Welcome to our film festival: Boston edition

Watch it outside Boston

Watch it ouside events and Co presents the first ever film festival showcasing films from 5th August to 8th August in Boston Public Garden, a park in Boston that can hold upto 1500.

Watch it outside events and Co endeavors to present a program of films that entertain, enlighten and educate the audience. We are dedicated to supporting evolving filmmakers by providing a forum to showcase their artistic vision. A variety of awards will be presented , i.e. Best Film, Performance, Documentary, and Lifetime Achievement. Categories have been added to laud productions that influence green industry living in addition to the unique "Mass Impact Award" given to a filmmaker whose movie illuminates a social issue that positively affects humanity.

The festival will offer audiences first-hand access to the filmmakers and filmmaking process during the Q&A sessions with the Director and talent following every screening. The watchitinboston will continue its commitment to being a valued member of the city's cultural community contributing to the desirability of residing and working in the Boston public garden. Further, a rich history of programming illustrious films and featuring top actors elevates the international visibility of the region via publicity.

Register all and don't miss this once in a life time event. Spend time with loved ones, enjoy food, wine and above all enjoy the variety of films we will be showcasing.
